Cheese Sticks

Description

These super-simple breadsticks are easy, cheesy, and there's no dough to make because we're going to use frozen puff pastry for this. They're very cool for entertaining.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 package frozen puff pastry
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil (preferably drained from tin of anchovies)
  • 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1/4 cup shredded sharp white Chedder cheese
  • 5 tablespoons freshly shredded Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Unfold dough dough into a flat sheet and brush with olive oil.
  3. Sprinkle cheeses onto the dough, covering the surface.
  4. Cut the dough down the seam lines into 9 pieces.
  5. Twist the dough strip from both ends 8 or 9 times.
  6. Sprinkle reamining cheese over the sticks.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 20-30 minutes, with a flip.
