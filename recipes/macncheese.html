Macaroni and Cheese

Description

This homemade mac and cheese recipe uses a classic mixture of sharp Cheddar and Parmesan. Cheddar is a fantastic base, while Parmesan adds a more complex and salty flavor. Of course, you can adapt this recipe to suit your taste: Gruyere, Monterey Jack, and Gouda are all great options for macaroni and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup salted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk, or more as needed
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Boil salted water, add macaroni. Drain, transfer to baking dish
  3. Melt 1/4 cup butter over low heat. Whisk in flour and stir.
  4. Whish 2 1/2 cups mlk into flour mixture. Pour sauce to macaroni.
  5. Make the bread crumb topping.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for about 30 minutes.
